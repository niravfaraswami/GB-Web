Support · 03

7-day no-question returns. 100% refund on damaged or defective.

Most kits never come back to us. When one does, here's exactly what happens: how to start a return, what's eligible, how long the refund takes by payment method, and what we do for the edge cases.

7 days · RETURN WINDOW5 working days · REFUND SLAFree, via DTDC · PICKUP
The short version. Damaged on arrival, defective, or wrong item → free pickup, full refund in 5 working days. Opened spice mix or started ferment → not eligible (hygiene). Anything in between → message us; we usually find a way.
The return process

Four steps. Five working days end-to-end.

Notify us within 7 days

WhatsApp +91 78619 40110, or email returns@gutbasket.com. Send your order number and a photo if there's damage.

Get RMA & shipping label

We email an RMA (return authorisation) within one working day. DTDC reverse-pickup is scheduled at no cost to you.

Pack & dispatch

Repack the unopened kit in the original carton if you have it. Tape the label on top, hand it to the DTDC agent. No print-out needed.

Refund processed

Once the kit reaches us and a quick condition check passes, refund is initiated. Most payment methods reflect within 5 working days; UPI is instant.

Eligible

What we take back.

  • ✓ Damaged in transitCracked jar, broken seal, leaking sachet
  • ✓ DefectiveSealing failure, mould visible inside sealed sachet, off-spec contents
  • ✓ Wrong itemDifferent SKU shipped, missing item from kit
  • ✓ Unopened & unusedWithin 7 days, original packaging, all sachets sealed
  • ✓ Failed ferment in 30 daysFree replacement kit (no return needed)
Not eligible

What we can't take back.

  • ✗ Opened spice mixHygiene — sachet seal is the freshness guarantee
  • ✗ Started fermentOnce you've added water and salt, the kit is "in process"
  • ✗ Tampered packagingResealed pouches, cut sachets
  • ✗ Past 7-day windowUnless defective — defects are 30 days
  • ✗ Change of mind on consumablesRefill packs and recipe books are non-returnable

Edge cases

Three situations the policy doesn't quite cover.

The courier marked it "delivered" but I never received it.
Message us within 24 hours of the marked-delivered timestamp. We'll open a courier investigation (DTDC has a 7-day SLA), and if the kit isn't recovered, we ship a free replacement on day 8. If you receive both eventually, just let us know — we'll send a return label for one.
My ferment failed at day 5 — is this returnable?
Within 30 days of order, yes. Failed ferments are usually a chemistry issue (room temp, wrong salt, contamination) and we'd rather replace your kit than argue about whose fault it was. Send a photo and the date you started, we'll send a fresh kit. We learn a lot from these reports.
I received a kit as a gift and it's not for me — can I return it?
Yes, with the gift-giver's permission (or if you can supply the order number). We'll refund as store credit by default — usable on any future GutBasket order. Cash refund only if the giver requests.
The recipe book is missing from my kit. Just send the book?
Done. Message us with your order number, we'll dispatch the recipe book separately within one working day, no return needed. Same for missing sachets, missing airlock, missing glass weight, etc.
Can I exchange a kit for a different SKU?
Yes if unopened & within 7 days. Process is the same as a return; once we receive your kit, we ship the new one same-day. Any price difference is settled either way (we refund or charge the difference).
