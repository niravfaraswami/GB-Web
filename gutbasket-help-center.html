Top FAQs

Six things people ask every week.

How long does the kit take from order to my door?
Orders placed before 4pm IST ship the same working day from Gandhinagar via DTDC. Metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, Kolkata, Ahmedabad): 2–3 working days. Tier-1: 3–4 days. Tier-2: 4–6 days. North-East & remote pin codes: 7–10 days. You'll get a tracking link on WhatsApp the same day we ship.
My ferment hasn't started bubbling — is it dead?
Probably not. The most common cause is room temperature below 22°C. LAB activity slows dramatically in cool weather; in winter we recommend keeping the jar near the kitchen (not on a cold floor or in an AC room). If after 4 days at 24–28°C there's still nothing, message us on WhatsApp with the date you started, the room temperature, and a photo of the jar.
There's white film on top of my ferment. Is it safe?
If it's a thin, white, dry-looking film (like a wrinkled skin), that's kahm yeast — harmless, just unsightly. Skim it off with a clean spoon and the ferment underneath is fine. If the growth is fuzzy, blue, green, or pink — that's mould; don't eat it, message us and we'll send a replacement kit.
Can I change my delivery address after ordering?
Yes, if the order hasn't shipped yet. Reply to your order confirmation email or WhatsApp us within 4 hours of placing the order. Once a tracking link is generated we can't change the address; in that case the parcel will be returned to us and re-shipped (no extra cost to you).
Are GutBasket kits safe for children?
Yes for kids 3+, with a few notes. Lacto-fermented foods are traditional in Indian households; the safety record is excellent. Start with milder ferments (achaar, plain kanji, sauerkraut) before introducing kimchi or aam panna which can be spicy. Reduce salt-brine concentration if a recipe lists 3% — 2% is fine for kid-leaning batches. Always serve chilled or at room temp; never reheat probiotic foods (heat kills LAB).
What's your return policy in one line?
7-day no-question returns on damaged, defective, or wrong items. Refund in 5 working days to the original payment method. Opened spice mixes and started ferments aren't returnable for hygiene reasons — but if a kit fails within 30 days through no fault of yours, we'll send a free replacement. Full policy on the Returns & refunds page.
