      TIPS
        YOUR FIRST MONTH

        WHAT TO EXPECT
        WEEK-BY-WEEK.

        DAYS 1–3
        SETTLING IN
        Start with a half-scoop with breakfast. Mild gas is normal — your gut is meeting fiber it isn't used to.
        WEEK 1
        REGULARITY
        Bowel movements become more predictable. Move to a full scoop (5g). Most early bloating fades.
        WEEK 2–3
        ENERGY SHIFT
        Steadier energy through the day. Less afternoon slump. Better gut bacteria = better nutrient absorption.
        WEEK 4+
        DAILY HABIT
        It just becomes part of breakfast. The gut microbiome shift is gradual — most studies show meaningful change at 4 weeks.
