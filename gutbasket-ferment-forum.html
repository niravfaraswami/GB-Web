Forum rules

Five rules. That's it.

  1. 01 Be kind. Beginners post here. So do food scientists. Same tone for both.
  2. 02 No medical claims. "I felt better after kanji" is fine. "Kanji cured my IBS" is not.
  3. 03 Photos help. Show, don't just tell. Most diagnoses happen from a single photo.
  4. 04 No commercial promotion. Other brands welcome to participate as members. Selling? Take it elsewhere.
  5. 05 Recipe credit. If you adapt someone's post, link to it. Forum culture matters.
Top contributors get the badge. The forum has three tiers: Beginner, Regular, Mentor. Mentors are voted by members each quarter and get free refills for life. We pay them in salt.
