QUESTIONS?

COMMON QUESTIONS

Why borosilicate glass instead of regular glass?+
Borosilicate handles temperature swings better — pour hot brine in, transfer to fridge, no cracking. Regular glass cracks under thermal shock.
Will it fit my existing kit?+
Yes if you have a GutBasket kit. For other brands, the wide-mouth thread is standard — fits 90% of mason jar accessories.
How do I clean it?+
Dishwasher safe (top rack). Or hand wash with hot water + mild soap. For stuck-on residue, soak overnight with baking soda.
Lifetime use — how long is "lifetime"?+
As long as the glass doesn't break. We've had customers using their original jars for 4+ years. The lid threads stay good for 5+ years.
Is the lid airtight?+
Yes when sealed properly. For active fermentation, you should attach an airlock instead — the airtight lid will pressurise from CO2 buildup.
Microwave safe?+
Yes — without the lid. Borosilicate handles microwave heat. Don't microwave with the metal lid attached.
Replacement parts?+
Lids and grommets are sold separately. Glass jar is rarely replaced unless dropped — we replace under warranty for manufacturing defects.
Storage when not in use?+
Air-dry completely before storing. Stack with lids loose to avoid trapping moisture.
